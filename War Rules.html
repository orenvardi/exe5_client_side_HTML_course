War Rules

The winter is coming!!!


You need to prepare yourself to the big war, a long journey ahead of you, full of enemies and dangers.


On each fight you will have 15 seconds to choose two strong partners, whis them you will fight against the enemy, if you are thinking that your partners are too week you can choose to run away.


Your partners and enemy scorred by:


  • Number of people they killed during the series
  • number of people guarding them
  • the house they are coming from
  • If the character was killed along the series, she will get weaker and if she survives the series, she will be stronger.

Notice!


If your rival killed one of your partners on the series (or the opposite), he will kill him even in the battle!
